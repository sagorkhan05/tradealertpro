FAQ

Common questions.

How does the 2-day free trial work? +
After registering, you get full access to all alerts for 48 hours — no payment or card required. After the trial, you choose a subscription plan to continue.
Which news events trigger alerts? +
Only High-Impact events from the Forex Factory economic calendar — such as NFP, CPI, Interest Rate Decisions, GDP, and other major releases. Low and medium impact events are filtered out.
How exactly are notifications delivered? +
The Chrome extension runs in the background and checks the calendar every minute. When a high-impact event is 5–10 minutes away, a Chrome system notification appears on your screen — even if you're on another tab.
Can I use it from outside Bangladesh? +
Yes. TradeAlert Pro works globally. Payment is via crypto so there are no geographic restrictions. The extension works in any country where Chrome is available.
What crypto coins can I use to pay? +
We accept USDT (TRC20/ERC20), BTC, ETH, BNB, TRX, and many more via NOWPayments. USDT TRC20 is recommended for the lowest fees.
Does it work when Chrome is minimized? +
Yes. As long as Chrome is running (even minimized), the background service worker keeps monitoring and will push notifications to your desktop.
